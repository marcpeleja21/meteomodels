About MeteoModels

A free, open tool that lets you compare the world's leading numerical weather prediction models — side by side, in real time.

🔭 What is MeteoModels?

MeteoModels is a weather forecast comparison app built for curious users, weather enthusiasts, and professionals who want to go beyond a single forecast. Instead of showing you one model's prediction, it shows you all of them — so you can see where they agree and where they diverge.

It runs entirely in your browser, loads fast, works offline (PWA), and requires no sign-up or installation.

✨ Features

🌡️Temperature, precipitation, wind, humidity & more — compare every model hour by hour
📊Ensemble plume charts: visualise forecast uncertainty across 141 model members
🚨Official weather alerts from MeteoAlarm (Europe) and NWS (US), filtered to your exact location
🗺️Interactive OpenStreetMap with nearby webcam view
📍Geolocation: get an instant forecast for your current position
🌐Available in Catalan, Spanish, English and French
📲Installable as a PWA — works offline, no app store required
